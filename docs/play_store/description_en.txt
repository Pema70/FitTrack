FitTrack – Your Daily Health & Fitness Companion

Track your calories, log your workouts, and discover low-calorie recipes — all in one app designed for Android.

KEY FEATURES:

📊 CALORIE & NUTRITION TRACKER
• Add meals quickly and easily
• Photo diary using your phone's camera
• Automatic calorie and macro calculation (protein, fats, carbs)
• View history by day using a calendar or arrows

🏋️ WORKOUT DIARY
• Choose from 10+ activities: running, gym, cycling, swimming and more
• Auto-calculate burned calories based on duration and your weight
• Manual calorie entry from your smartwatch or sports device

🍽️ LOW-CALORIE RECIPES
• Browse a curated recipe database
• Filter by calories and ingredients
• Add, edit and delete your own recipes
• Save favourites for quick access

👤 PERSONALIZED PROFILE
• Enter your age, gender, weight and height
• Automatic daily calorie goal calculation (BMR/TDEE via Mifflin–St Jeor)

🔔 NOTIFICATIONS & OFFLINE MODE
• Hydration reminders throughout the day
• Full functionality even without internet connection

PRIVACY & SECURITY
Passwords are stored using BCrypt hashing. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Your data belongs to you.
